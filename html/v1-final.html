Dedication Drives Us

It is difficult to describe a typical Weber State student. Our students vary widely in age, background and life experience. They might be fresh out of high school, or have a family, or a job (or two, or three). They might be starting something new, or finishing what they began years ago. But here’s what they share: They each have a dream, and they have the grit and tenacity, the dedication and determination, it takes to make that dream happen.

At Weber, we’re proud of our welcoming, student-focused environment and are excited to help you succeed. As an open-enrollment university, we offer the opportunity of higher education to anyone who wants to pursue a dream. Do you have that hope? That spark? Turn your ideas into action!

Shouting from a Mountaintop

“My campus is prettier than yours.” Our students are constantly posting photos with that caption. And for good reason: Weber’s mountainside setting is inspiring, and we like to say it offers “mountains of opportunities.” Whether you’re seeking solitude or craving a challenge, you’ll find four seasons of recreation on the peaks, trails, rivers and reservoirs that surround this active, outdoorsy city.

Skiing or biking? Paddling, climbing or hiking? Get the gear and expertise to get out there!

Connections Strengthen Us

Weber has been deeply rooted in our community for more than 130 years. Our students have a strong spirit of service, dedicating well over 100,000 hours of community service annually. Our athletics teams unite our university and community in Purple Pride. And our arts and educational programs and events make us a cultural hub for the region. Weber is a place to come together: Our doors, and minds, are open.
